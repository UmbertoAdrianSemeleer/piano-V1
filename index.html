About Frère Jacques

Frère Jacques, also known as "Brother John" or "Are You Sleeping?", is a popular French nursery rhyme. It originated in France in the 17th or 18th century and has since been translated into many languages worldwide.

The song is often used to teach basic musical concepts and is one of the first songs many children learn to play on the piano.
